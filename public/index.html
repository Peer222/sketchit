Tutorial

  1. Click on 'New Painting'.
  2. The painting is visible for 3 seconds.
  3. Noise image is shown for 1 second.
  4. Sketch on the canvas.
  5. Submit your sketch when its finished.
  6. Click on 'New Painting' to sketch the next.

If you think you can't sketch the painting. Click 'New Painting' to get a new one.

Click on 'Erase' to remove unwanted strokes and click 'Sketch' to start sketching again.

You can look up the painting as many times as you want by clicking 'Show Painting'.
Important: After the image is displayed again, the canvas is cleared.
